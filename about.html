About Us

Introduction

Welcome to the official online platform of Darul Ifta, headed by Mufti Saleem Ullah. Our core mission is to provide authentic, well-researched, and accessible Islamic rulings (Fatawa) to Muslims around the world based on the Quran, Sunnah, and classical Islamic Jurisprudence.

Our Vision

In a rapidly changing world, new challenges emerge daily in fields like finance, medicine, and social interactions. Darul Ifta aims to bridge the gap between traditional Islamic scholarship and contemporary issues, providing clarity and peace of mind.

About Mufti Saleem Ullah

I am an Islamic scholar, Mufti, and lecturer with over 9 years of experience in issuing fatawa, teaching Islamic sciences, and providing Shariah-based solutions to contemporary issues. Having completed my Dars-e-Nizami and Takhasus fil Ifta from Jamia Binoria Al-Alamia, Karachi, I have authored and reviewed hundreds of fatawa under the supervision of senior scholars.

In addition to my expertise in Islamic jurisprudence, I serve as a khateeb, delivering sermons and guiding communities on social and ethical matters. I have also completed a Qadha course (Islamic judiciary and arbitration) and a certification in career counseling, enabling me to bridge modern professional guidance with Islamic values.

I am also a Hafiz and Qari of the Qur’an, actively engaged in Qur’an teaching, tajweed training, and spiritual development programs.

My passion lies in making Islamic scholarship accessible and relevant to the challenges of modern life, combining tradition with contemporary needs in education, research, and community service.
