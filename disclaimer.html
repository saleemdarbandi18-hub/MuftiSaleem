Legal Disclaimer

The information and Fatawa provided on this Darul Ifta platform are strictly religious opinions based on the principles of Islamic Jurisprudence (Fiqh). They are not intended to serve as legally binding verdicts in a court of law in any secular jurisdiction.

Application of Fatawa

A Fatwa is issued based solely on the phrasing and specific scenario described by the questioner. Changing a minor detail might alter the ruling completely. Readers should not apply a Fatwa to their own situation without ensuring absolute parity, and if in doubt, they should submit a specific question themselves.

Medical & Financial

Fatawa related to medical procedures or financial investments outline the Shariah permissibility. They do not constitute professional medical advice or certified financial advisement. Always consult registered professionals in those respective fields.
