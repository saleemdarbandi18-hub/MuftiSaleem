Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Information Collection

We respect your privacy. The only information we collect from you is what you voluntarily provide through our "Ask Question" form, which may include your name, email address, and question content.

Data Usage

The information collected is used solely for the purpose of communicating the Fatwa directly back to you. The content of your question may be published publicly on our website as a generic Fatwa, but we will strictly anonymize all personally identifiable details (names, specific locations, etc.) to ensure complete privacy.

Cookies

We may use basic analytical cookies to understand how our website is used and to improve the user experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.
